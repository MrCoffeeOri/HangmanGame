How to Play Hangman

  • Guess the hidden word by typing one letter per box.
  • Click Check Word or press Enter to check your guesses.
  • You have a limited number of errors before the game ends.
  • Use Get Hint (or press Shift + H) to reveal a letter (once per round).
  • Use Arrow keys to move between letters.
  • Press Shift + N or click New Game to start over.

Good luck and have fun!
